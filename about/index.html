About Tycoons Investments

Tycoons Investments is an Egyptian real estate brokerage focused on helping buyers compare available projects and units before speaking with a sales consultant.

The website combines live inventory, AI-assisted search, project pages, area pages and direct WhatsApp follow-up. Our goal is to make property decisions clearer by showing price, area, delivery, finishing and payment-plan information in one place.

How we work

Inventory is collected from developer materials, availability sheets, brochures and direct sales updates. Property availability and prices can change, so final confirmation is always completed with the Tycoons sales team and the relevant developer.

Our approach

We prioritize practical comparison: what fits the buyer’s budget, intended use, preferred location, delivery timeline and investment objective.
